Climate Change

Causes:

  • emission of greenhouse gases, for example:
    • burning fossil fuels
    • agriculture
    • steel making
    • forest loss

Fact:

  • The concentration of carbon dioxide (CO2)​​​​​​​ in our atmosphere, as of July 2021, has reached the peak in human history1
  • 11% of emissions of all global greenhouse gas emissions caused by humans are due to deforestation2
  • 800 million people (~10% of the world's population3) are vulnerable to climate change impacts such as droughts, floods, heat waves, extreme weather events and sea-level rise4
